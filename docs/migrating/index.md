# Migrating

Coming from another parser? Each guide below opens with what carries over
automatically, maps the old tool's concepts to their nParse+ equivalents,
and ends with what you gain. The
[comparison page](../comparison.md) has the full feature matrix.

- **[From nParse](from-nparse.md)** — your config migrates automatically,
  custom timers included.
- **[From GINA](from-gina.md)** — no importer (honest!), but the trigger
  concepts map one-to-one; recreating your key triggers is quick.
- **[From EQTool](from-eqtool.md)** — near-identical feature set by
  design; mostly a matter of finding where things live.

And whichever you come from: nParse+ speaks EQTool's network protocol, so
your old groupmates on EQTool see your map dots and shared timers as if
nothing changed.
